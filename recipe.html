Tofu Miso Soup

INGREDIENTS

  • Miso Paste
  • Water
  • Hondashi
  • Tofu
  • Dried wakame(Sea weed)

INSTRICTIONS

  1. Boil water
  2. Cut tofu into small pieces
  3. When water is boiled, put hondashi, tofu and dried wakame
  4. Remove from the heat and add miso
  5. Put back on the heat until it settles down
  6. Ready to eat!
